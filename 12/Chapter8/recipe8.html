Protein Powder Pancakes

Ingredients
1 cup oat flour 1 scoop protein powder
1 teaspoon baking powder 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup 1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract Pinch of salt
Instructions
1. In a bowl, mix oat flour, protein powder, and baking powder.
2. Add milk, honey or maple syrup, egg, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Stir until well combined.
3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan for each pancake.
4. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side.
