Ingredients:

  • 1 serving of protein powder
  • 1 banana
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of oats
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup Greek yougurt

Instructions:

  1. Blend all ingredients together
  2. Heat a pan on medium heat
  3. Grease the pan with coconut oil
  4. Pour the batter into the pan
  5. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface
  6. Flip the pancake and cook for another minute
  7. Repeat until all the batter is used
