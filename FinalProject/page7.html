Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos, born on January 12, 1964, is an American business magnate, media proprietor, investor, and commercial astronaut. He is the founder of Amazon, the world's largest online retailer, and served as its CEO until stepping down in 2021.

Bezos played a pivotal role in shaping e-commerce and revolutionizing the retail industry. Under his leadership, Amazon expanded its services beyond online retail, including cloud computing, streaming, and artificial intelligence.

In addition to his business ventures, Bezos is known for his interest in space exploration. He founded Blue Origin, a private aerospace manufacturer and spaceflight services company, with the goal of making space travel accessible to civilians.
