Mark Zuckerberg

Mark Zuckerberg, born on May 14, 1984, is an American computer programmer and Internet entrepreneur. He is best known as the co-founder and CEO of Facebook, one of the world's largest and most influential social media platforms.

Zuckerberg, along with his college roommates, launched Facebook from their dormitory at Harvard University in 2004. The platform quickly grew into a global phenomenon, connecting people worldwide and transforming the way we communicate and share information.

In addition to his role at Facebook, Zuckerberg has been involved in various philanthropic efforts, aiming to address social issues through the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative.
